How to export your session cookies

The Octopus Energy dashboard uses a login system that requires a real browser session. Export your cookies once and paste them below — they usually last several weeks. You only need to repeat this when the scraper reports that your session has expired.

  1. Install the Cookie Editor browser extension: Chrome  /  Firefox
  2. Open octopus.energy in your browser and sign in to your account.
  3. Click the Cookie Editor icon in your toolbar.
  4. Click Export → Export as JSON (the cloud ↑ icon).
  5. The JSON is now in your clipboard — paste it into the field below.
  6. Click Save Cookies.

Tip: Make sure you export cookies for the octopus.energy domain (not auth.octopus.energy). Cookie Editor should show these automatically when you're on the octopus.energy page.
